Oats

Description

A filling low calorie breakfast option.

Serve with peanut butter and/or cinnamon for added taste.

Ingredients

3 cups water

1 cup oats

Half teaspoon salt

Cooking Method

Combine water and oats in a pot and place on stove.

Allow to cook for 5-10 minutes then add salt, stir, and reduce heat

Cook for another 5-10 minutes and remove from the stove.

Nutritional Facts (100g cooked)

Calories

63

Carbs

10.8g

Protein

2.6g
