Chicken

Description

A tasty source of protein, and sometimes fat.

Ingredients

The chicken

Some cooking oil (not too much)

Salt and seasoning

Water

Cooking Method

Fill a stainless steel steamer pot with about 1 inch water and bring to a boil.

Add chicken into the pot, cover, and add salt. Cook for 10-15 minutes.

Once water is done, lower heat and add oil and seasoning. Lightly fry till brown on all sides
