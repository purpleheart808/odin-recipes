Boiled Corn

Description

A filling low calory leafy vegetable.

Goes well with just about any savory dish.

Ingredients

The kale

Cooking oil

Salt

A tomato

An onion

Cooking Method

Preheat a pan and add a little oil to it.

Add chopped tomato and onion to the pain and lightly fry till soft.

Add salt and chopped kale to the tomato and onion and cook till kale is soft but still green.

Nutritional Facts (100g cooked)

Calories

130

Carbs

31g

Protein

4.2g

Fat

1.1g

Notes

May need to account for calories of oils/condiments added during cooking process
