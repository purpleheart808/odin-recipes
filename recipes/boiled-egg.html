Boiled Egg

Description

A super low calorie source of protein and fat.

Good as a snack or served as part of a savory meal.

Ingredients

Eggs

Water

Salt

Cooking Method

Fill a stainless steel steamer pot with some water and bring to a boil.

Add eggs into the pot, sprinkle some salt and cover, and reduce heat. Cook for 10-15 minutes depending on how you want the yolk.

Nutritional Facts (1 large boiled egg)

Calories

78

Carbs

0.6g

Protein

6g

Fat
