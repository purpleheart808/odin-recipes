Boiled Egg

Description

the perfect hard-boiled egg is not boiled at all but rather steamed!

This secret was leaked by a chef, and it is the best way I've found for easy peeling and no green yolks.

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs

Steps

  1. Fill a stainless steel steamer pot with about 1 inch water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add eggs to the steamer tray, carefully lower into the pot, cover, and reduce heat. Cook for 10 minutes for medium yolks or 15 minutes for fully-cooked yolks.
  3. Add eggs to the steamer tray, carefully lower into the pot, cover, and reduce heat. Cook for 10 minutes for medium yolks or 15 minutes for fully-cooked yolks.
  4. Gently crack each egg, roll on a cutting board, then peel the shell off from the fat end towards the point.
